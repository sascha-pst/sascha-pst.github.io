About

The human
behind the data.

I spent six years at Meta designing and scaling education programs for creators and small businesses across global markets — writing content that reached millions monthly and building systems that made complex tools feel approachable. That experience taught me that the most powerful thing you can do with information is make it usable.

Now I'm deepening my technical foundation through Berkeley's Master of Information and Data Science program, where I focus on machine learning, data engineering, and statistical modeling. I'm drawn to the intersection of rigorous analysis and human-centered storytelling — and especially to using both in service of economic empowerment, climate, and AI for social good.

Outside of work you'll find me at the pottery studio (currently obsessed with nerikomi), on a yoga mat, or somewhere between Beijing and Vancouver. I think the best teams are built on trust, directness, and genuine curiosity about each other's work.
