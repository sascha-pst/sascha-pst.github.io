You make me eat porridge for breakfast. I don’t yet know who I am.

You didn’t leave for another country. I’m still keeping the secret.

You sit next to me every day and never speak. Nearly a decade passes. You sit next to me and never speak.

You pick me up outside the apartment. We ride the motorcycle to dinner. I never found your obituary.

You came on time to the restaurant. We share a bottle of wine. I stand on the subway by myself.

Your father would be at our wedding. I wouldn’t have held your hand at his funeral.

I didn’t move cities. We chose us instead of me.
